How Flux works

Three simple steps to organized feedback

1

Client submits feedback

Clients use a clean form to describe issues, attach files, and set priority. No training needed.

2

Agency reviews & discusses

Your team reviews feedback on the Kanban board, adds comments, and moves items through the workflow.

3

Approve & convert to tasks

Approved feedback becomes actionable tasks assigned to team members with deadlines.
